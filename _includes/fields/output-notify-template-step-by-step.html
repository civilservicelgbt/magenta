Step 1 of 7

Log in to GOV.UK Notify

Open GOV.UK Notify in a new window and log in.

If you do not have access to the Network's GOV.UK Notify account, contact the Chair.

Step 2 of 7

Add a new template

On the GOV.UK Notify service, create a new email template.

Step 3 of 7

Copy the template name

Copy the template name, using the button below.

Paste it into the Template name field on the GOV.UK Notify service.

Step 4 of 7

Copy the email subject

Copy the email subject, using the button below.

Paste it into the Subject field on the GOV.UK Notify service.

Step 5 of 7

Copy the email message

Copy the email message content, using the button below.

Paste it into the Message field on the GOV.UK Notify service.

Step 6 of 7

Save the template

On the GOV.UK Notify service, save the email template using the Save button.

Step 7 of 7

Your template is ready to use

You now need to use the template to send the email.

If you have access to the mailing list data, use the “Send the email” step-by-step guide below. If you do not have access to the mailing list data, please contact the Chair to send the email on your behalf.
